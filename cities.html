Mumbai

Mumbai is a populated city. On the Mumbai Harbour waterfront stands the iconic Gateway of India. Elephanta Island holds ancient cave temples dedicated to the god Shiva. The city's also famous for the Bollywood film industry.

Delhi

Delhi, India’s capital territory. In Old Delhi, a neighborhood dating to the 1600s, stands the imposing Mughal-era Red Fort, a symbol of India, and the sprawling Jama Masjid mosque. Nearby is Chandni Chowk, sweets shops and spice stalls.

Chennai

Chennai is the capital of the state of Tamil Nadu. The city is home to Fort St. George, and now a museum showcasing the city’s roots . Religious sites include Kapaleeshwarar Temple, and St. Mary’s, a Anglican church.

Kolkata

Kolkata is the capital of India's West Bengal state.Kolkata is home to West Bengal's film industry Tollywood. It’s also home to Mother House, headquarters of the Missionaries of Charity, founded by Mother Teresa.
